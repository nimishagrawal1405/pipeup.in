01

Who We Are

PipeUp is a free creator community platform for Indian student content creators. We are an early-stage platform operated by three founders — Nimish Agrawal, Krishna, and Adarsh — based in India. PipeUp is currently not a registered company, LLP, or formal legal entity. We operate as an informal team and are working toward formal registration.

PipeUp connects student creators with brands for paid collaborations, provides access to community workshops, and facilitates creator-to-creator collabs — all through our website PipeUp.in and our WhatsApp-first community.

⚠ Early Stage Notice

PipeUp is in its early stage. Processes, policies, and the platform itself are actively evolving. We will always try to communicate changes clearly and honestly.

02

Who Can Join

PipeUp is open to creators who produce content primarily for Indian audiences. There is no minimum follower count — we care more about niche fit and genuine intent than raw numbers.

Eligibility — Creators

  • You must be a student or recent graduate creating content for Indian audiences.
  • You must be located in India or creating content targeted at Indian audiences.
  • You must provide accurate information in our intake form, including your social handles, niche, city, and contact email.
  • If you are under 18 years of age, you must have explicit parental or guardian consent before joining (see Section 9).

Eligibility — Brands

  • Brands must be legitimate businesses with real products or services.
  • Brands must fill out the brand intake form truthfully.
  • Brands must intend to pay creators fairly for their work.
  • PipeUp reserves the right to decline any brand that does not align with our community values.
03

How PipeUp Works

PipeUp acts as a full intermediary between creators and brands. Here is what that means in practice:

For Creators

  • You submit your details via our form on PipeUp.in and are redirected to our WhatsApp community.
  • When a suitable brand deal becomes available, we will reach out to you directly on WhatsApp.
  • Matching is done manually by our team based on niche, audience fit, and brand requirements — not purely on follower count.
  • Joining PipeUp does not guarantee you will receive a brand deal. We make best-effort matches but cannot promise specific outcomes or timelines.
  • Community features (workshops, collabs) are free and open to all members.

For Brands

  • Brands submit a form expressing interest in working with creators.
  • Our team personally reads each form and manually matches brands with suitable creators.
  • Deals are coordinated by PipeUp, including negotiating deliverables, timelines, and payment terms.
  • All payments are made to PipeUp first, and we distribute the creator's share after deducting our platform fee.

PipeUp is a community-first platform. We are not a talent agency, a course platform, or a marketplace. We are a team that genuinely cares about helping student creators get real opportunities.

04

Payments & Fees

PipeUp manages all financial transactions between brands and creators. Below is how payments work:

Step Who What Happens
1 Brand → PipeUp Brand pays the full agreed deal amount to PipeUp before the campaign begins.
2 PipeUp (internal) PipeUp deducts 30% as a platform/service fee.
3 PipeUp → Creator Creator receives 70% of the deal amount after content delivery confirmation.

Payment Timeline

Creator payments will be processed within a reasonable time after the brand confirms delivery of the agreed content. PipeUp will communicate the expected timeline clearly for each deal.

No Hidden Charges for Creators

Creators are never charged any upfront fee to join PipeUp or access the community. The 30% fee is only applicable when a deal is successfully made, and it is deducted from the brand payment — you always receive your share net of the fee.

Payment Method

Creator payments will be made via UPI, bank transfer, or any mutually agreed payment method. Creators must provide accurate payment details to receive their earnings.

Important

PipeUp does not charge creators any fee to join, access workshops, or participate in the community. The 30% platform fee only applies to brand deal earnings and is deducted before the creator's payment is disbursed.

Brand Deal Liability

Because PipeUp manages the full deal — collecting payment from the brand and paying out to the creator — we take on a meaningful level of responsibility in the process.

PipeUp's Responsibility

  • PipeUp is responsible for collecting payment from brands before a deal begins. Creators will not be asked to start work until payment is secured from the brand.
  • If a brand fails to pay after agreeing to a deal, PipeUp will work to resolve the situation and will not require the creator to absorb that loss.
  • PipeUp is responsible for clearly communicating deal terms, deliverables, and deadlines to both the brand and the creator.

Creator's Responsibility

  • Once you accept a deal, you are expected to deliver the agreed content on time and as per the agreed brief.
  • If you fail to deliver without a valid reason, PipeUp reserves the right to withhold your payment, require a refund to the brand, or remove you from the platform.
  • You must disclose all brand partnerships on your social media as required by ASCI (Advertising Standards Council of India) guidelines. This is your legal responsibility.

Disputes

All disputes between creators and brands will be handled by PipeUp's team as the intermediary. Our decision in such matters, while made in good faith, is final in the context of the PipeUp platform. We are not a court of law and our liability is limited to the value of the specific deal in question.

Limitation of Liability

PipeUp's total liability in any dispute is capped at the value of the specific brand deal involved. PipeUp is not liable for indirect losses, loss of future opportunities, or reputational damage arising from a brand deal that goes wrong.

Content Rights

When you create content as part of a brand deal facilitated through PipeUp, the rights to that content are shared between you and PipeUp as follows:

Brand Deal Content

  • Content created for a brand deal is owned jointly by PipeUp and the creator.
  • PipeUp reserves the right to use, repurpose, repost, or share this content for platform promotion, portfolio building, case studies, brand pitches, and marketing materials — without additional payment to the creator.
  • The brand also receives a license to use the content for the duration and scope agreed in the deal brief.
  • You retain the right to post the content on your own channels.

Non-Deal Content

Any content you create independently — that is not part of a PipeUp-facilitated brand deal — remains 100% yours. PipeUp has no claim over your personal creative work.

Attribution

When PipeUp reposts or shares your brand deal content, we will make reasonable efforts to credit your handle. However, for aggregate case study materials or pitch decks shared privately with brands, individual creator attribution may not always be possible.

Summary

If PipeUp got you the deal, PipeUp can use that content. Everything else you create is fully yours.

Minors Policy (Under 18)

PipeUp is open to creators under the age of 18, but with additional requirements to protect younger members.

Parental Consent Requirement

  • If you are under 18 years of age, you must have the explicit consent of a parent or legal guardian before joining PipeUp.
  • By submitting our form as an under-18 creator, you confirm that your parent or guardian is aware of and has approved your participation in PipeUp.
  • For brand deals, a parent or guardian must be informed about and approve each deal before work begins.
  • PipeUp reserves the right to request proof of parental consent at any time.

Payment for Minors

Payments for deals completed by under-18 creators may be processed to a parent's or guardian's bank account or UPI ID, subject to applicable Indian laws regarding minors and contracts.

Legal Note

Under Indian Contract Act, 1872, minors cannot enter into binding contracts. All brand deal agreements involving creators under 18 must be co-signed or acknowledged by their parent or legal guardian for the agreement to be valid.
