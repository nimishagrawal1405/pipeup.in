why free

we're not free
because we're broke.

we're free because we believe every student creator deserves a shot — not just the ones who can afford a ₹15,000 course or have the right connections.

pipeup will stay free for creators. always. we'll build a sustainable model by connecting brands to you — not by charging you to get there.

who it's for

any creator.
any follower count.

If you're showing up, learning, and trying to get better, you belong here. Pipeup is for creators at the beginning, in the middle, and still figuring it out.

01
Start before you feel ready
You do not need a big audience, polished content, or brand deals already in hand. The point is to learn in public and grow with people around you.
02
Learn from creators at every stage
Beginners get clarity. Growing creators get support. Everyone gets access to practical lessons, better systems, and a more useful community.
03
Build real momentum together
This is not just content about growth. It is a place to ask questions, share work, meet collaborators, and keep going when progress feels slow.
